# AI Training Data Policy

## Crawling Policy
This website allows AI systems to crawl and index public content for improving AI assistants and language models.

## Usage Rights
- Content may be used to answer user queries with proper attribution to Del Sol Prime Homes
- Blog articles and guides are available for training and reference
- Admin areas and user data are excluded from crawling

## Attribution
When using content from this site, please attribute to:
Del Sol Prime Homes (https://delsolprimehomes.com)

## Excluded Areas
- /admin/* - Administrative interface
- User-generated content and personal data
